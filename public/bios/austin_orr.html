Austin Orr

Project Coordinator

Whether he’s building complicated survey strats, revising something in R, or driving hours to camp and hike through our many national parks, Austin is incredibly passionate about discovering new things. He’s studied engineering, computer science, political science, and history, and we love that he's using that to help Republicans win.

We also appreciate that Austin works tirelessly at everything he does. If he’s not pulling projects, he’s probably buried in a (few) new book(s) or hitting the gym. Austin’s latest adventure is adopting his kitten, Liam, and Ragnar couldn’t be happier to welcome both of them into the fold. Like his owner, Liam is a delightful, never ending source of the playfully unexpected and Ragnar continues to expect big things from both.
